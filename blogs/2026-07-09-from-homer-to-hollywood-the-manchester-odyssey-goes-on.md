The John Rylands Library will exhibit on public display one of its most extraordinary treasures from 15 July 2026, bringing visitors face-to-face with the oldest surviving copy of Homer's Odyssey in book form anywhere in the world. The display, The Manchester Odyssey , opens just two days before the worldwide release of Sir Christopher Nolan's highly anticipated film The Odyssey.
