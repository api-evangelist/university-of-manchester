The Manchester Institute of Innovation Research hosted the R&D Management Conference 2026 in Manchester, attracting over 600 global delegates to explore innovation amid technological disruption. This event marked the conference's return to its origin city, emphasizing creativity and resilience in research and industry. The conference included a doctoral colloquium with 30 PhD students, fostering academic career development and peer networking.
