More than 140 guests gathered at Alliance Manchester Business School on 30 June for a special event with Bank of England economists and authors Rupal Patel and Jack Leslie , celebrating the publication of their new book, Money: The Inside Story . Hosted by Professor Fiona Devine , Vice-President and Dean of the Faculty of Humanities, the event brought together business leaders, policymakers, students and members of the public for a lively discussion on one of the most important – and often misunderstood – forces shaping everyday life: money.
